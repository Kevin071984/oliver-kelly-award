What to Expect

Scripts are accepted on a rolling basis. There are no cycles or deadlines — submit when you're ready.

We read every play. If your work is selected, we will reach out to the email address provided. The review process can take several months.

If you have not heard from us within six months, your play was not selected for this cycle, and you are welcome to submit a new script.

What We Accept

All formats are welcome: solo shows, 10-minute plays, one-acts, and full-lengths. We are looking for excellence in writing — not production-readiness.

Musicals are not accepted.

Your Rights

Submitting your script does not transfer any rights to The Oliver Kelly Award. You retain full ownership of your work. If selected, we feature your play in our archive with your name and attribution.

The Prize

Winning playwrights receive $20 via Venmo or PayPal. We'll be in touch with the winner directly to arrange payment.

Winning playwrights may submit again after one month from the date their play was selected.

The Submission Fee

The $10 fee helps sustain the award and fund prizes. It is non-refundable. Payments are processed securely by Stripe.
